CURRENTLY ONLY FOR WINDOWS! THIS MAY ALSO CAUSE PERFORMANCE ISSUES FOR THOSE WITH SLOWER HARD DRIVES!

Download: 
https://github.com/alex-mireles/gcc-input-display/releases/

How to use:
	Open Dolphin and go to controllers on the top bar
	On the bottom under "Advanced Settings" turn on "Write Gamecube Controller Inputs To File"
	When you run the game dolphin will generate a file named "GCCinputs.bin" in the root of your dolphin folder
	Open gcc-input-display.exe from the input display download and select "File Path"
	Navigate to where your dolphin.exe is and double click "GCCinputs.bin"
	Done!
